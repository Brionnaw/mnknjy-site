mnknjy derives from my native name Monkonjay, meaning for your business. Originally the line started out strictly as a lingerie line and was under a different name. Through the personal growth of myself is how mnknjy became to be.

mnknjy is a premier leisurewear line made from local organic fabrics. We strive for sustainable designs and ethical practices.

We like to say this brand is a product of the Bay Area. The process of creating every aspect of us has derived heavily from the Bay Area. For instance, our dye guy has a shop in Novato, our patternmaker resides in Oakland and has a studio in Berkely, our packaging designer lives in Walnut Creek, Our web designer in Richmond and our two lovely seamstresses do business in Oakland. As for me, I stay in the itty bitty city by the water, Vallejo. Not to mention we source our fabric through a San Francisco company that partners with local California farmers.
This brand is truly Bay Area driven.

As stated above we're a small batched company. Currently, we have two seamstresses to supply inventory for the website. We decided to go this route because fast fashion and mass producing is not something we are fond of. With fast fashion they follow trends, they make excess items, they're always trying to keep up.

For us product is made in accordance to you the customer. Customer feedback is taking into consideration and held in the highest regard. It's because of you that we are here so we listen to your needs and wants.

All items are released in capsule collections that consist of anywhere between 4-10 pieces and are released in exclusive colors that I've created at my grandmother's kitchen table with primary paints.

Simplicity is key. We're a minimal brand.
All fabrics are 100% organic, each item is sewn by one person.

We're giving you comfort in style.
